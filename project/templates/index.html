Welcome to chatGPTeacher!

Why

We believe that effective learning starts with engaging, interactive practice. However, we know that creating or finding practice questions can be a time-consuming or difficult task for students and teachers alike.

Our solution

With chatGPTeacher, you can upload your course materials, like lecture slides, and create a quiz that tests your knowledge of the material. Our platform provides real-time feedback on your performance, allowing you to identify areas where you may need further study. By using our website to create quizzes, you'll improve your understanding of course material and achieve better grades.

Get started

Ready to get started? Upload your PDF file, click "Create Quiz" and start generating a customized quiz.
